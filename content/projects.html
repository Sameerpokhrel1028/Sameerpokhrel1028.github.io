Research

Ongoing PhD Research

Current research integrating plant breeding, genomics, and pangenome-based analysis for peanut improvement.

Pan-MAGIC Platform (Lead)
18 divergent peanut lines developed into 8-way and 16-way MAGIC populations; pangenome construction, variant detection, and population-scale genomic analysis.
Development of Drought-Tolerant Peanut Lines (Lead)
Breeding and evaluation of drought-tolerant peanut germplasm; 2–3 highly drought-tolerant lines ready for release as germplasm.
Development of White Mold–Resistant Peanut Lines (Lead)
Breeding and genomic analysis for white mold resistance; expected germplasm release in Spring 2027.
TSWV Resistance Loci Identification (Collaboration)
Identification and characterization of loci associated with Tomato spotted wilt virus resistance.
Wild Introgression & Nematode Resistance Gene Discovery (Collaboration)
Mapping introgressed genomic regions and candidate gene discovery for nematode resistance.
Novel Tar-Spot Disease Fungus Identification (Collaboration)
Identification and characterization of a novel tar-spot disease fungus.
Long-Read Low-Pass Sequencing Technology Advancement (Collaboration)
Development of cost-effective long-read, low-coverage sequencing strategies for breeding applications.
