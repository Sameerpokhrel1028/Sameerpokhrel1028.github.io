Research

My research focuses on peanut (Arachis hypogaea) breeding and genomics, with an emphasis on drought tolerance, disease resistance, and pangenome-based approaches for trait discovery.

  • Development of a population-specific pangenome for a 16-way MAGIC peanut population
  • Introgression detection and structural variation analysis using graph-based genomics
  • Integration of field phenotyping, GWAS, and bioinformatics pipelines
  • Development of an R Shiny application to visualize genome-wide variant density

Live Shiny app: Variant Density Explorer
