About

I work on peanut (Arachis hypogaea) breeding and genomics with an emphasis on pangenomes, introgression detection, and trait mapping for drought tolerance and disease resistance.

I build reproducible bioinformatics pipelines (HPC + R/Python) and interactive visualization tools for genome-wide variant density and genome clustering.

Research Projects

Peanut pangenome graph construction and variant projection
Introgression detection and region-level variant profiling
Shiny app for variant density heatmaps and genome clustering

Live app: Variant Density Explorer
